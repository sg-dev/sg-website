Welcome to the

Chair of Systems Design

We are a group of about 15 researchers from various disciplines (statistical physics, applied mathematics, computer science, political science and economics). Our research can be best described as data driven modelling of complex systems with particular emphasis on social, socio-technical, and socio-economic systems.
