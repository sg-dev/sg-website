## Just published in **Science**: <br> How malicious AI swarms can threaten democracy

 A disruptive threat is emerging: Swarms of collaborative,
 malicious AI agents. Fusing LLM reasoning with multiagent
 architectures, these systems are capable of
 coordinating autonomously, infiltrating communities, and
 fabricating consensus efficiently. By adaptively mimicking
 human social dynamics, they threaten democracy.
