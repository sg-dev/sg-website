Dr.

Luca Verginer

Postdoc

Luca Verginer is an economist working on subjects, covering

  • Fundamentals of Temporal Networks
  • Science of Science
  • Research Policy
  • Supply and Distribution Networks

To tackles these problems he relies on a wide variety of methods including but not limited to

  • Social Network Analysis
  • Econometrics
  • Machine Learning (e.g. NLP, Graph Neural Networks)
  • Agent Based Modelling

Prior to joining Chair of Systems Design Team, Luca received a Master’s degree in Economics from the University of Trento and a PhD in Economics and Data Science from IMT School for Advanced Studies, Lucca.

Luca is als a core contributor to Pathpy, an open source temporal network analysis library developed in collaboration with the DAG group University of Zürich and the University of Wuppertal.

The work on the exciting and novel field of temporal networks allows are is central in addressing temporal economic network data (e.g. Supply Chains).
