Registration

The workshop will take place in the ``Physikzentrum'' Bad Honnef.

Thanks to the generous sponsoring by Wilhelm and Else Heraeus Foundation, there is no conference fee for participating at the international workshop "Economic Dynamics from the Physics Point of View", and accomodation in the ``Physikzentrum'' will be provided for free.

However, due to the limited capacities there, the total number of participants is limited to 60 persons altogether. Thus, your participation cannot be guaranteed.

You can apply for participation in the workshop by using our

Interactive Form

If the number of applications exceeds the possibilities, a selection among the applications needs to be made.

If your application has been accepted, you will receive a corresponding letter directly from the Heraeus Foundation, which will also indicate whether you may present a poster or a contributed talk.
In any other case, you will be informed by e-mail around February 1, 2000.
