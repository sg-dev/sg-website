Conference Site

The international workshop ``Economic Dynamics from the Physics Point of View'' will take place in the

Physikzentrum Bad Honnef

Located in the middle Rhine valley near the German cities of Bonn and Cologne, this is a nice old castle-like Conference and Meeting Place of the German Physical Society (DPG) - so the the workshop takes place ``in the heart of the physics community''.
